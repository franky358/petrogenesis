Marine Fuel Quality.

VIP supplies the highest quality, compliant marine fuels to its customers. In accordance with the Global Allowable Sulfur Content Limit of 0.50% set by the International Maritime Organization (IMO), which came into force and applied as of January 1, 2020, our marine fuel (fuel oil) supplies comply with the Low Sulphur Regulation (VLSFO).

To ensure quality control and eliminate our customers' concerns, operations, logistics, strict and thorough control of Vision International Petroleum's entire supply chain, product purchase and fuel delivery comply with ISO 8217:2017 specifications. We implement high and strict quality controls throughout the supply chain through sampling, testing and certification, complying with the best practices and procedures in the industry for the handling and safeguarding of the product.

As quality is a continuous process, it is extremely important to monitor the handling, the standards, the procedure of where, when and how the product is handled from origin (refinery-tanks) to delivery-loading to the ship, no matter how many times it is verified. For us, it is necessary to guarantee quality and a successful service in line with the supply needs of our customers.

VIP offers its customers a wide variety of physical drop-off points in the State of Texas. In addition, the team provides a close link between trading and operations to ensure that the service is performed as efficiently and effectively as possible.

Fuel quality.

We have developed business relationships with the most important refineries in the state of Texas, oil companies and marketers/distributors around the world to have access to the highest quality fuels and the most economical prices in all our locations.

VIP supplies the highest quality fuels that meet current American and Mexican standards for its customers. In accordance with the minimum permissible sulfur limit of 15 ppm and maximum sulfur content of 500 ppm Diesel ULSD and in gasoline the minimum permissible sulfur limit of 30 ppm and maximum sulfur content of 80 ppm, established by the governing bodies in Mexico, our fuel supplies comply with low sulfur content regulations.
